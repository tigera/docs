### Before you begin

- Verify your cluster meets [requirements]({{site.baseurl}}/get-started/requirements/system-requirements)

- Review your install options

  **Note**: You cannot change the install method for a cluster after initial installation.

  - **Default** - uses `kubectl` commands 
  - **Private registry** - uses {{site.prodname}} images uploaded to a private registry
  - **Helm** - uses Helm v3 charts

### Install {{site.prodname}}

1. In Manager UI, click **Managed Clusters**.
1. Click **Connect Cluster**. 
   
Follow the steps for your install option. 

#### Default 

1. Add the **Cluster Name**, and select the **Cluster Type**.
1. Click **Connect** and follow the wizard.
1. Monitor the progress on the Managed Clusters page.

#### Private registry

1. Follow the steps to add {{site.prodname}} images to a [private registry]({{site.baseurl}}/get-started/connect/setup-private-registry).
1. When you are done, come back to the **Managed Clusters** page.
1. Click **Connect Cluster**. 
1. Add the **Cluster Name**, and select the **Cluster Type**.
1. Click **Advanced Options**, and select both **Install via helm** and **Install private registry**. (Private registry supports only Helm).
   ![private-registry]({{site.baseurl}}/images/private-registry.png)
1. Complete the fields, ensuring that your inputs match the values used when setting up your private registry.
     >**Note**: If your private registry requires credentials, create the **calico-cloud** namespace on your cluster and then create an image pull secret and use the same name in the **Registry Secret Name**.
     {: .alert .alert-info}
1. Click **Connect** and follow the wizard.
1. Monitor the progress on the Managed Clusters page.

#### Helm

1. Click **Connect Cluster**.
1. Add the **Cluster Name**, and select the **Cluster Type**.
1. Click **Advanced Options**, and select **Install via helm**.
   ![helm]({{site.baseurl}}/images/helm.png)
1. Click **Connect** and follow the wizard.
1. Monitor the progress on the Managed Clusters page.
