To upgrade managed clusters to the latest version of {{site.prodname}}:

1. In Manager UI, go to **Managed Clusters**.
1. Highlight the cluster to upgrade in the list.
1. Click the **Reinstall** icon.
1. Copy the command and apply it to appropriate cluster.
